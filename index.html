The Labyrinth of Sins

Are You Scared Yet?

The Labyrinth of Sins is a blend of darkness, horror, and endless turns! Find the keys and escape, if you can! The Labyrinth was created as a final project for CSIS 380, Computer Graphics. This project was created using the Agile software development technique over the course of 4 one week sprints. We are proud to finally be releasing our game.

    Min Sys Requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows
  • Graphics card: Intel Integrated Graphics 5000 or AMD equivalent
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • Processor: Intel I3 or AMD Equivalent
  • Hard drive space: Approximately 150 MB
